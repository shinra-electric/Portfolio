About Us

Seventy years bringing floral arrangements to the city of Limerick

Mill Flowers has been in existence since the 1950's. Three generations of our family have work in the family business, artistically hand-arranging flowers for every occasion. We provide services from everyday bouquets, birthday and holiday gift baskets, funeral baskets, special occasion arrangements, and more! No matter what the occasion, Mill Flowers will put every ounce of love, dedication and personalisation to every piece that is created by us.
